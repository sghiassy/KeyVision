How It Works

Three steps to a smarter key collection

KeyVision's pipeline is designed to be fast, robust, and fully automatic — no manual tagging or barcode scanning.

1

Photograph the key

Point your phone at any key on a flat surface. The app handles cropping, orientation, and blur detection automatically.

2

AI creates a fingerprint

DINOv2 vision AI converts the image into a 768-dimensional embedding — a unique signature for that key's shape, cuts, and profile.

3

Recognize instantly, offline

Hold up any key and the app tells you what it is in real time — even with no internet. Confidence scores show you exactly how certain the match is.
